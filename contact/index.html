Get in touch

Contact & find us

We are at the end of the Marenport quay, where the harbour meets the open sea. Write to us, call us, or simply come and find the light in the windows.

A word before you write

We read every message

Whether you are planning a celebration, asking about a dietary need, or simply curious about a dish you read about in the journal, your note lands with a real member of our team rather than an inbox no one watches. We aim to reply by email within a day, and often the same evening once service has quieted down.

For the fastest answer to a booking question, the reservation form is usually quickest, since it reaches our maître d' directly. For everything else - press, careers, private events, or a kind word about a recent dinner - the form below is the surest way to reach us. We would genuinely love to hear from you.

Getting here

Valet parking is available from 5 pm at the foot of the quay. The step-free entrance is on Rue Albany. The harbour bus stops two minutes' walk from our door.

Find us at the seaward end of Quai des Lumières, beside the old harbour light.
