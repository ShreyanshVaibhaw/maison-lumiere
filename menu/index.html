Dinner service

The Maison Lumière menu

Our card is rewritten each afternoon around what the boats and the garden bring. The selection below is a faithful sample of a typical evening this season.

Starters

  • Bay scallops, citrus beurre blanc $28

    Day-boat scallops seared in brown butter, finished with Meyer lemon and sea herbs.

  • Heirloom tomato & burrata $22

    Terrace tomatoes, creamy burrata, basil oil and toasted sourdough from the morning bake.

  • Chilled pea velouté $19

    Garden peas and mint folded with crème fraîche, finished with a coil of cured ham.

  • Marenport oysters, mignonette $24

    A half-dozen oysters from the bay with shallot mignonette and lemon.

Mains

  • Maple Hills duck, cherry & thyme $42

    Slow-rendered duck breast with a glaze of harbour cherries, thyme and aged sherry.

  • Whole roasted sole, brown butter $46

    Line-caught sole roasted on the bone with capers, parsley and buttered new potatoes.

  • Garden vegetable cassoulet $34

    Slow-cooked white beans, late-summer vegetables, thyme and a crisp herb crust.

  • Dry-aged beef, bordelaise $52

    Grass-fed ribeye aged in-house, red-wine bordelaise and pommes purée.

Desserts

  • Tahitian vanilla soufflé $18

    A featherlight soufflé poured table-side with crème anglaise and salted caramel.

  • Stone-fruit tarte fine $16

    Wafer-thin pastry, caramelised apricots and house lavender ice cream.

  • Dark chocolate & sea salt $17

    Single-origin chocolate délice, olive oil, flaked sea salt and cocoa nib.

Wine & cellar

  • Loire Sauvignon Blanc, by the glass $14

    Crisp, mineral and built for our shellfish - the sommelier's house pour.

  • Côtes du Rhône rouge, by the glass $16

    Supple and peppery, an easy companion to the duck and the beef.

  • Coastal pairing flight (four wines) $58

    A guided flight matched to the season's menu by sommelier Tomas Vance.

  • Cellar list Ask

    Two hundred bins from French growers and a few coastal neighbours. Please ask your server.

Tasting menus are available for the full table from Wednesday to Saturday. Please note any allergies when you reserve.
