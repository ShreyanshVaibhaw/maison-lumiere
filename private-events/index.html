Gather with us

Private events by the sea

From an intimate anniversary to a full-room celebration, we host gatherings with the same care we bring to every evening service.

The setting

A room made for occasions

Maison Lumière was built for moments worth marking. The same candlelight, the same harbour view, and the same quiet attentiveness that define our nightly service become the backdrop for your celebration - only the table is yours alone.

We host gatherings of every size, from a private corner for ten to an exclusive buyout of the entire thirty-eight-seat dining room. Whatever the occasion, our events team plans every detail with you, from the first canapé to the last glass of dessert wine.

How we host

Three ways to celebrate

The Quay Table

A semi-private corner of the dining room for up to twelve guests, with a bespoke set menu and an optional wine pairing chosen by our sommelier. Ideal for birthdays and family dinners.

From $95 / guest

The Lumière Room

Our private salon seats up to twenty around a single table, with its own service team and a tailored multi-course menu. A favourite for milestone anniversaries and corporate dinners.

From $135 / guest

Full buyout

Take the entire maison for the evening - all thirty-eight seats, the terrace, and the undivided attention of the kitchen. Perfect for weddings and once-in-a-lifetime celebrations.

By arrangement
Every detail

Planned with you

Each event begins with a conversation. We will talk through your occasion, the people you are gathering, and the feeling you are hoping to create - then we build the evening around it.

Our team can arrange bespoke menus and dietary accommodations, a wine pairing for the table, floral styling, printed menus bearing your name, and a sequence of service tuned to speeches or quiet conversation. Nothing is off the shelf.
