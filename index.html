Our story

A small room with a long horizon

For more than fifteen years we have cooked from a single, sunlit kitchen looking out over the Marenport harbour. The dining room seats thirty-eight guests, and every evening begins the same way: a glance at what the morning boats and the kitchen garden delivered, and a menu rewritten in pencil.

We believe a restaurant should feel like an invitation rather than a performance. The silver is polished, the bread is warm, and the welcome is genuine - the rest is simply good cooking, served at its own pace.
